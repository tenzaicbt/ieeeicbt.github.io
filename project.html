Upcoming Meeting Announcement - IEEE Student Chapter

Date: December 10, 2024

Time: 10:00 AM

Location: ICBT Main Branch Auditorium

We are excited to invite all students to the upcoming IEEE Student Chapter meeting! This event will provide an excellent opportunity to learn more about IEEE, its benefits, and how you can get involved.

What is IEEE?

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world’s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. As an IEEE student member, you will have access to a wide range of resources, career development tools, networking opportunities, and a global community of professionals and students in various technical fields.

What to Expect at the Meeting:

Introduction to IEEE and its core values

Benefits of being an IEEE student member

Upcoming IEEE events and initiatives

Opportunities for networking and personal growth

Q&A session to answer all your queries
